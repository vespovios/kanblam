# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 12 July 2026_

KanBlam is operated by **Computability Ltd** ("we", "us"). This page explains
what data the hosted service at kanblam.com collects, why, and what your
rights are. Questions or requests: **privacy@kanblam.com**.

The short version:

- **No ads, no tracking cookies, and we never sell data.**
- **The app itself is not instrumented.** Once you're signed in, nothing you
  do — boards, tasks, comments — is analytically tracked or recorded.
- Analytics run **only on the public pages** (landing, docs, login, signup),
  are cookieless, EU-hosted, and capture no personal information.
- **Self-hosting KanBlam sends us nothing.** Your server, your data.

## What we collect, and why

**Account data.** Your email address, display name, and password. Passwords
are stored only as bcrypt hashes — we cannot read them. Used to sign you in
and connect you to your workspace.

**Workspace content.** The projects, tasks, subtasks, comments, tags,
holidays, and settings you (or agent members you connect) put on your boards.
This is your data; we store and process it solely to provide the service to
you and never for any other purpose.

**API tokens.** Personal and agent tokens are stored as SHA-256 hashes. The
plaintext token is shown to you once at creation and never retained.

**Transactional email.** Workspace invitations and sign-in links are sent to
the email addresses involved via Resend, our email delivery provider. We
don't send marketing email to account holders.

**Waitlist (pre-launch).** Joining the waitlist sends your email address to
us as a notification email. Our analytics records only the domain part
(e.g. `gmail.com`) — never the address itself.

**Billing.** Paid hosted plans are processed by Polar.sh as merchant of
record. Your card details go to Polar and their payment processor — they
never touch our servers. We store your workspace's subscription status
(plan, trial/active/lapsed, renewal dates).

**Analytics — public pages only.** We use PostHog (EU region) to count visits
to the public pages: the landing page, docs, login and signup. It runs
cookieless (in-memory only), records no session replays, and captures no
personal information. The authenticated app is never instrumented.

**Infrastructure logs.** kanblam.com is served through Cloudflare, so
requests transit their network (including your IP address) for TLS, caching
and DDoS protection, subject to Cloudflare's own processing terms. Our
servers keep short-lived operational logs for debugging and abuse
prevention.

## Cookies

- **Session cookie** — essential; keeps you signed in. No session cookie is
  set until you log in.
- **`kb_currency`** — remembers the pricing currency you picked on the
  landing page.
- That's all. No analytics or third-party tracking cookies.

## Where your data lives

The hosted service runs on servers in the **EU**, and analytics data stays in
PostHog's EU region. The processors we use:

| Processor | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Contabo (EU) | Server hosting |
| Cloudflare | TLS, proxy, DDoS protection |
| Resend | Transactional email |
| Polar.sh | Payments (merchant of record) |
| PostHog (EU) | Public-page analytics |

## Retention and deletion

- Workspace data is kept for as long as your workspace is active.
- **Demo workspaces** (try.kanblam.com) are throwaway by design and are
  automatically deleted within about 24 hours.
- Deleting content in the app (tasks, projects, workspaces) removes it from
  the live database immediately.
- To delete your account and all associated data, or to request a copy of
  your data, email **privacy@kanblam.com** — we'll complete the request
  within 30 days.

## Your rights

If you're in the EU/EEA or UK, the GDPR gives you the right to access,
correct, export, and erase your personal data, and to object to or restrict
its processing. Email **privacy@kanblam.com** to exercise any of these. You
also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory
authority.

## Self-hosting

KanBlam is open source (AGPL-3.0). A self-hosted instance communicates only
with your own infrastructure: there is no phone-home, no telemetry, and
analytics are off by default (with no PostHog key configured, no analytics
code even loads). This policy applies to the hosted service at kanblam.com,
not to instances others run.

## Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update this page and the date at the top.
Material changes to how we handle account data will be announced to account
holders by email.
