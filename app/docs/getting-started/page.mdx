# First login

Your first 60 seconds inside KanBlam, in order.

![DayDash, the daily dashboard you land on after logging in](/images/docs/daydash.png)

## The top bar

After you log in you land on **DayDash** — your daily dashboard
([full guide](/docs/daydash)). Across the top:

- **The workspace name** on the left, next to the KanBlam logo.
  Click the logo any time to return to DayDash.
- **Filter chips** — Project, Assignee, Quadrant, Tags, Hide-completed.
  Once set, they cascade across every view (Tasks, Kanban, Calendar,
  Eisenhower). Click **Reset filters** to clear all of them.
- **Quick add (`+`)** for creating a task in seconds. Keyboard shortcut
  `⌘K` (macOS) or `Ctrl+K` (Windows/Linux) — works from any page.
- Your avatar circle in the top-right. Click it to sign out.

## The tabs

Just below the top bar, the page tabs:

- **DayDash** — your daily summary, what needs you today.
- **Projects** — list of all projects, click any row to open one.
- **Tasks** — full task table with sort + filter.
- **Kanban** — drag-and-drop card board grouped by stage.
- **Calendar** — month and week views, drag to reschedule.
- **Eisenhower** — Important-vs-Urgent matrix.
- **Tags** — manage tags and their colours.
- **Settings** — account, workspace, team, working days, holidays.

## Your first task

Three ways to create one:

1. **Quick add** — press `⌘K`, type `Fix login [WEB] #auth due:fri !high`,
   hit Enter. KanBlam parses the brackets/hash/exclamation hints.
   See [Keyboard shortcuts](/docs/keyboard) for the full syntax.

   ![The Quick Add palette parsing tokens as you type](/images/docs/quick-add.png)
2. **Tasks → + New task** — opens the full new-task dialog with every
   field (description, subtasks, recurrence, etc.).
3. **From a project page** — click any project, then the Tasks tab,
   then **+ New task**. The new task is pre-assigned to that project.
